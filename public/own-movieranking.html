Ultimate movie list

My top 3 movies of all time

La La Land

This is the ultimate comfort, fun, masterpiece of a movie. Damien Chazelle really went sicko mode on this one. The Cinematography is insane like every frame is beautiful. Even if you don't like musical, this movie WILL make you like musical.

Before Sunrise

This is the second movie of the "Before Trilogy". Even though the first movie is more famous and iconic, it's still not as amazing as this movie. What I love about it is the simplicity and the fact that it's very minimalistic but still delivers.

Almost Famous

This is a new favorite and it made its jump to my top 3. I can't really pin point why I like this movie so much, I guess I was just very immersed in the storystelling, it's like I was the main character
